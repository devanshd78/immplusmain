Questions? Look Here.

Some questions about Immplus are asked frequently. We've answered the most frequent of those frequent questions below

  • Start Your Health Journey with Immplus

    Take the first step towards better health with Immplus. Our platform is designed to provide quick access to quality healthcare solutions, from medicine delivery to specialist consultations and lab testing. Whether you're seeking preventive care or specialized treatments, Immplus has you covered with services that prioritize your well-being and convenience.

  • How does Immplus ensure quick medicine delivery?

    At Immplus, we prioritize fast delivery by partnering with local pharmacies and utilizing advanced logistics technology. Our dedicated delivery personnel ensure that your medications arrive within 15 minutes.

  • What types of medicines can I order through Immplus?

    Immplus offers a wide range of medicines, including prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs, and health supplements. We strive to have a comprehensive inventory to meet your healthcare needs.

  • Is it safe to order medicines online?

    Yes, ordering medicines through Immplus is safe. We ensure that all products are sourced from licensed pharmacies and adhere to strict quality control standards. Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

  • What if I need a prescription for my medicines?

    If your medicine requires a prescription, you can upload it directly through our app during your order. Our pharmacists will verify the prescription before processing your order.

  • What is Immplus' refund policy?

    At Immplus, we prioritize the safety and satisfaction of our customers. Please review your order thoroughly upon delivery. If you find any damage, incorrect items, or expired medicines, **do not accept the order**. Only share the OTP with the delivery partner once you have checked and verified the medicines are correct and in good condition.

    For any issues, please contact Immplus customer support immediately to assist with replacements or any further questions.
